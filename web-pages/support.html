Frequently Asked Questions

How do subscriptions work?

Pro and Business subscriptions are billed through your Apple App Store or Google Play account and will automatically renew each billing period until you cancel. You can cancel your subscription anytime in your store settings. To restore a previous purchase, visit the Paywall or Profile screen in the app.

How do AI features use my photos?

When you choose to use an AI feature, your photos are securely sent to trusted third-party AI services only to create your specific results—such as outfit analysis, styling recommendations, or virtual try-ons. Your photos are never used for training AI models, advertising, or facial identification. Learn more in our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account anytime from the app by going to Profile → Delete account. Alternatively, you can request deletion by emailing support@fancypot.org. For more details, visit our account deletion page.

I hit my monthly limit

Usage limits reset monthly (try-ons reset weekly). To get higher limits and access more features, upgrade to Pro or Business directly in the app. These plans give you more generous allowances and exclusive styling tools.
