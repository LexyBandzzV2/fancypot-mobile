Delete your Fancy Pot account

How to delete your account

In the app

Go to Profile → Delete account and type DELETE to confirm. Your account will be permanently deleted immediately.

By email

Send a deletion request from the email address associated with your account to support@fancypot.org with the subject line "Delete my account". We'll process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When you delete your Fancy Pot account, the following information is permanently removed from our servers:

• Account credentials and login information
• Closet photos and images
• Saved outfits and collections
• Style preferences and personal settings
• AI usage history and generated results

Important: If you have an active subscription (Pro or Business), you must cancel it separately in your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings. Deleting your Fancy Pot account does not cancel store billing.

Privacy & compliance: Minimal records may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
