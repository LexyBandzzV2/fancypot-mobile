Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Fancy Pot ("we," "us," "our") builds an AI-powered wardrobe and styling app available on the web and as iOS and Android apps. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. If anything here is unclear, write to us at support@fancypot.org — we're happy to explain.

1. Information we collect

We collect only what's needed to run Fancy Pot and make your styling experience better:

Account information

  • Email and password — used to create and secure your account, via Supabase Auth.
  • Display name (optional) — shown in the app if you choose to add one.
  • Phone number (optional) — used only for account verification.

Photos you choose to upload

  • Clothing photos and outfit photos you add to your digital wardrobe.
  • A full-body photo, optionally, if you use our virtual try-on feature.

Style information

  • Style preferences, favorite stores, and budget ranges you set, used to personalize recommendations.

Usage & account records

  • AI feature usage records (which feature was used, associated cost, and timestamps), so we can operate usage limits and your subscription correctly.
  • Subscription tier and status (e.g., Free, Pro, Business).
  • Standard security logs, including IP address and device/browser user agent, used for rate limiting and abuse prevention.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Create, secure, and maintain your account.
  • Provide core features: wardrobe organization, outfit generation, piece recommendations, and virtual try-on.
  • Personalize recommendations based on your stated style preferences.
  • Process subscriptions and enforce tier-based usage limits.
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Communicate with you about your account or respond to support requests.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use third-party advertising networks or tracking technology to follow you across other companies' apps or websites.

3. Third-party AI processing

Some Fancy Pot features rely on third-party AI service providers to do the actual image processing. Here's exactly what that means:

  • When you use a feature that requires AI (clothing item analysis and background removal, outfit generation, piece recommendations, or virtual try-on), the specific photo(s) you submit for that feature are sent to a third-party AI service provider solely to generate the result you requested.
  • We ask for your explicit, in-app consent before the first time any photo is sent to an AI provider. You'll see a clear explanation before you can use these features.
  • Your images are not used by us for advertising, are not used to train our own machine learning models, and are not used to identify or profile people.
  • You can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting support@fancypot.org. Withdrawing consent disables AI-powered features, but you can keep using the rest of the app (like manual wardrobe organization) as normal.

4. Storage & payment processing

Your account data, photos, and preferences are stored and processed using Supabase (authentication, database, and private file storage). Photos are kept in private storage buckets and served to your device only through short-lived, signed URLs — they are not publicly accessible.

Payments are handled by trusted third parties, not by us directly:

  • Web: payments are processed by Stripe.
  • iOS and Android: subscriptions are billed through the Apple App Store or Google Play, using RevenueCat to manage subscription status tied to your account. We never see your full card number on mobile.

5. Sharing of information

We share information only with the service providers necessary to run Fancy Pot, and only for the purposes described in this policy:

  • Supabase — hosting, authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Third-party AI providers — solely to generate the AI results you request (see Section 3).
  • Stripe / RevenueCat / Apple / Google — to process subscription payments.

We do not sell personal data to anyone, and we do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers. We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of Fancy Pot and its users.

6. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account is active, so the app can function properly.

You can delete your account at any time:

  • In the app: Profile → Delete account, or
  • On the web: fancypot.org/delete-account.

Deleting your account permanently removes your account data, photos, outfits, style preferences, and AI usage history, except for minimal records we retain where required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all traffic between your device and our servers.
  • Row-level security on our database, so your data is only accessible to you.
  • Encrypted session storage on your device.
  • Private storage buckets for photos, accessed only via short-lived signed URLs.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information using industry-standard practices.

8. Children's privacy

Fancy Pot is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact support@fancypot.org and we will take appropriate action.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal data, including the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data (see Section 6).
  • Port your data to another service in a structured format.
  • Object to certain processing of your data, including withdrawing AI consent (see Section 3).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@fancypot.org and we will respond as required by applicable law.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We'll update the "Last updated" date above, and where changes are material, we'll provide additional notice (such as an in-app message).

11. Contact us

Questions, concerns, or privacy requests? Reach us at support@fancypot.org.
